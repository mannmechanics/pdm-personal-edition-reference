# PDM Specification Stack v1.0

This directory contains the published specifications for Progressive
Depletion Minting (PDM) and the Mann Mechanics framework, as of
April 2026.

## Documents

### PDM_Foundation_Paper_v1_0.pdf

Institutional foundation paper. The front-of-funnel document for
first-time institutional readers. Covers the structural problem PDM
addresses, the mechanism in plain language, three worked sector
examples (carbon, central bank, energy grid), the institutional
framework, the engagement pathway, and a founder's note.

### PDM_Technical_Overview_v1_0.pdf

Mid-layer technical bridge between the Institutional Brief and the
Personal Edition Whitepaper. Suitable for senior policy advisors,
chief economists, and technical reviewers seeking a structured
orientation before reading the formal whitepaper.

### PDM_Telemetry_Companion_Paper_v1_0.pdf

Oₙ as Auditable Ledger. Telemetry integrity specification for the
inputs to the PDM control law. Covers fixed ontology, dual-key
validation, temporal expiry, netting, cryptographic attestation,
separation of powers, and constrained override protocol. Safe
Creative reference 2604245385991, timestamped 24 April 2026.

### PDM_Licensing_Framework_v1_0.pdf

Three-layer licensing and certification model: open study, commercial
licensing through Mann Mechanics Ltd, and independent institutional
certification through MannCert Ltd. Safe Creative reference
2604245386219, timestamped 24 April 2026.

### Mann_Mechanics_Institutional_Brief_v1_0.pdf

Reference document for ministers, central banks, and senior policy
leaders. Diplomatic register. Describes the framework, the engagement
pathway, and the structured engagement model.

### Mann_Mechanics_Institutional_Engagement_Fee_Schedule_2026.pdf

Published fee structure for Stage 1 (Institutional Briefing), Stage 2
(Sector Analysis), and Stage 3 (Implementation Blueprint) engagements.
Effective 1 April 2026 to 31 March 2027.

### Humanity_Allocation_Transition_Roadmap_v1_0.pdf

Long-term economic stewardship specification. Defines how the
framework's economic benefit is allocated between Founder Allocation
and Humanity Allocation, how that allocation transitions over time
conditional on certified institutional implementation, the geographic
ring-fencing of Humanity Allocation funds, the dynamic-denominator
transition equation, and the successor-state rules that protect the
framework's integrity. The transition mirrors the discipline of PDM:
conditionality, boundedness, progressive transition, and auditability.

## Related canonical documents (located elsewhere in this repository)

The PDM Personal Edition Whitepaper
(PDM_Personal_Edition_Whitepaper_v1_0_1.pdf) is the formal technical
specification, containing the full state transition function, proofs
of the structural invariants, and Appendix C empirical verification
across seven test regimes. It is the canonical technical reference
and remains in its existing location in this repository. Safe Creative
Work ID 2601084210286, timestamped 8 January 2026.

The PDM Personal Edition Reference Simulator and its Supporting
Documentation (browser-based reference implementation, regime
sequencer, 625-point parameter stability sweep) are also located in
this repository in their existing folders.

## Reading order

For first-time institutional readers:

1. Foundation Paper (institutional introduction)
2. Institutional Brief (engagement pathway, diplomatic register)
3. Technical Overview (mechanism in depth)
4. Personal Edition Whitepaper (formal specification with proofs)
5. Telemetry Companion Paper (integrity framework)
6. Licensing and Certification Framework (governance and certification)
7. Humanity Allocation Transition Roadmap (long-term stewardship)
8. Institutional Engagement Fee Schedule (engagement terms)

For technical reviewers:

1. Personal Edition Whitepaper (formal specification with proofs)
2. Reference Simulator and Supporting Documentation (in this repository)
3. Technical Overview (orientation across the stack)
4. Telemetry Companion Paper (integrity framework)
5. Foundation Paper (institutional context)
6. Licensing and Certification Framework (governance)
7. Humanity Allocation Transition Roadmap (stewardship model)

Both routes converge on the same complete picture; the order of
approach is a matter of preference.
